SIP Calculator - Frequently Asked Questions

Are SIP investments tax-free?

No, SIP investments are not tax-free. However, certain SIPs like Equity Linked Saving Schemes (ELSS) offer tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

What is the Interest Rate of SIP?

The interest rate of SIP investments varies depending on the mutual fund scheme chosen and market conditions. It's not a fixed rate.

What is the maximum tenure of a SIP?

The maximum tenure of a SIP depends on the mutual fund scheme, but typically it can range from 5 to 10 years or more.

Can I modify my SIP amount?

Yes, you can modify your SIP amount by submitting a request to your mutual fund company or through their online platform.

How to extend SIP duration?

You can extend the duration of your SIP by contacting your mutual fund company and submitting a request for the extension.

Can I estimate my SIP return online?

Yes, there are various online SIP calculators available that can help you estimate your SIP returns based on different parameters such as investment amount, tenure, and expected rate of return.
